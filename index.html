Specializing in portrait and event photography, Daniel Prado is a reliable photographer based in Long Island and New York City. He has worked with growing families capturing both candid and posed moments, individual portraits, and a variety of events.


Daniel is known for a clean, bold style that focuses on natural expression and thoughtful composition. Driven by a passion for visual storytelling and creative collaboration, he uses his skills and production resources to create images that feel authentic, polished, and meaningful for every client.
